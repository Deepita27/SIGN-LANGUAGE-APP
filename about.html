About SignTales

SignTales is a platform designed to bridge communication barriers for the deaf and mute community. Our mission is to make sign language learning accessible, engaging, and fun for everyone.

Tagline:

SignTales: Language is Seen, Not Just Heard

The Idea

We aim to make communication inclusive by providing an interactive learning experience for sign language. Through step-by-step lessons, real-time feedback, and video tutorials, we empower users to master sign language at their own pace.
